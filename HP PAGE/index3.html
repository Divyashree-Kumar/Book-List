Harry Potter

by J.K.Rowling

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone is the first book in J.K. Rowling’s famous fantasy series. It follows the story of Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is taken to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes new friends like Ron and Hermione, learns magic, and uncovers secrets about his past. At Hogwarts, Harry faces challenges, solves mysteries, and ultimately confronts the dark wizard Voldemort, who is trying to return to power using the magical Sorcerer’s Stone. The book combines adventure, friendship, magic, and the classic battle between good and evil, introducing readers to a rich and imaginative world.
