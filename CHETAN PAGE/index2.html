The Three mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The 3 Mistakes of My Life is a contemporary novel by Chetan Bhagat that follows the lives of three friends—Govind, Ishaan, and Omi—set in Ahmedabad. The story revolves around their dreams of starting a sports and cricket shop and how their personal ambitions, relationships, and unexpected events shape their lives. Through Govind’s narration, the book explores themes of friendship, passion, business struggles, communal tensions, and the challenges faced by youth in modern India. Each of Govind’s “three mistakes” becomes a turning point that impacts not just him but everyone around him, leading to emotional and life-changing consequences. The novel blends drama, romance, cricket, and social issues, making it a fast-paced and relatable read for young audiences.
